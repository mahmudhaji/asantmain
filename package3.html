3Days 2night Safari

Serengeti & Ngorongoro Crater from Zanzibar

ITINERARY HIGHLIGHTS.

DAY 01: ARUSHA – SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK

An early morning departure at 06:30 am from Zanzibar Airport to Arusha Airport. After arrival at Arusha airport, you will be picked up by our Safari guide to our 4x4 safari land cruiser. After breakfast, we head towards the Serengeti National Park, passing through the beautiful high lying farmland of Karatu and the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. We arrive in time for lunch and enjoy an afternoon game drive in the Serengeti National Park. Dinner and overnight at our public campsite.

DAY 02: FULL-DAY GAME DRIVE SERENGETI

After breakfast, you will do a game drive in Serengeti National Park with your lunch box, following the animal migration at the Northern Serengeti. You will be able to see wildebeest, zebra, topi, hartebeest, eland, antelope, hyena, and varieties of birds. After having your lunch, you will continue with the game view in Serengeti heading to the gate of Serengeti to check out. After that, you will proceed with a game en route heading to Ngorongoro Crater.

DAY 03: NGORONGORO CRATER – ZANZIBAR

After breakfast, descend into the crater with a lunch box, enjoying the game drive for more than six hours. The Ngorongoro Crater is one of the most densely crowded African wildlife areas in the world and is home to an estimated 30,000 animals. Later in the afternoon after satisfactory game drives, you will be taken through Arusha town and transferred further to Kilimanjaro International Airport for your evening flight back to Zanzibar Island where your safari ends.
